Medical Insurance Cost Analysis and Prediction

Comprehensive analysis of medical insurance cost drivers to provide actionable insights for healthcare planning and insurance policies.

Description: This project investigates factors influencing medical insurance costs by analyzing demographic and lifestyle data like age, gender, BMI, smoking status, region, and number of children.

Objectives: Identify cost-influencing factors and predict medical expenses using multiple regression analysis.

Technologies Used: Python (Pandas, Scikit-Learn, Statsmodels), Multiple Regression Analysis, Data Visualization.

Outcome: The analysis provides insights to manage healthcare costs and guide insurance policies, emphasizing factors such as smoking, BMI, age, and regional variations.
